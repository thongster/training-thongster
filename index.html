Training Thongster

Not only is it a physical journey, it arguable a more mental journey and a battle within my own mind. I'm practicing the discipline it takes to reform oneself physically. Although this is Training Thongster and this is very much about my physical activites, it is my mental thoughts and wavelengths which will be trained the most.

I must first control the mental in order to change the physical. My physical training and my body transformation is only a measure of how discplined I have become and who I am inside as a man.
